Who Am I?

I'm just a guy from LA who loves to code.

...Seriously.

There is just something about diving head-first into a seemingly endless pool of libraries and piecing together a technically sound, elegantly constructed, unique solution that I can't get enough of.

It is in that state of mind that I thrive.

Through programming, I get to utilize both my engineering background and creative disposition to conjure up an app from an idea in my head, kind of like a mad scientist .

It also has the added benefit of allowing me to meet immensely talented individuals that I can not only work with, but also learn from, ad infinitum.

And boy, do I also love to learn.

When I'm not wearing down the semicolon key on my keyboard, you can catch me training for my next half marathon , messing around on my loop station, somewhere in Azeroth , or lying down in the grass looking up at the stars. Because aren't they just so gosh darn beautiful?
